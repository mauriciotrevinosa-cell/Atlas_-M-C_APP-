Atomic Model

Asset as Atom

Each asset is an atomic system. The company = nucleus (protons + neutrons, structural stability). Price = electron (probabilistic motion). Valid class: ETFs, commodities, blue-chip stocks.

nucleus → structural identity
electron → price behavior
States

Electron States & Energy

Price doesn't occupy a fixed position — it exists in states. More energy (money in motion) → more accessible states → more volatility → wider bracket.

E↑ → states↑ → σ(price)↑
Probability

Probability Cloud

Price is a cloud, not a point. The cloud defines where price can be found. Observation reveals probability, not certainty. We know likelihood, not location.

P(x) = probability density
∫P(x)dx = 1 over state space
Exclusion

Nodes — Where Price Cannot Exist

The cloud contains nodes: zones of zero probability. Structurally impossible states. The model defines exclusion zones, not exact outcomes.

P(x_node) = 0 (structural)
Transitions

Quantum Tunneling

Rare transitions between states that don't follow continuous paths. Market gaps, crashes, squeezes. Probability is low, not zero.

P(tunnel) ≈ 0 but ≠ 0
→ gaps, crashes, squeezes
Superposition

Superposition of Outcomes

Multiple outcomes coexist simultaneously. No single outcome dominates until energy redistribution occurs. Schrödinger interpretation: where it can be, not where it is.

Ψ = Σ cᵢ|stateᵢ⟩
until energy resolves
Simulation

State Space (Cubes)

Each cube = a full simulation with hidden internal dimensions. Only the final state is projected. Dimensional compression via marginalization allows thousands of simulations to coexist.

Cube(d₁,d₂,d₃) → Project(terminal)
marginalize hidden dims
Symmetry

Matter / Antimatter

Every possible state has a mirrored counterpart. Most mirrors have probability → 0. Symmetry exists mathematically, not observationally. Ensures completeness of state space.

∀ state S, ∃ S̄ (mirror)
P(S̄) → 0 for most S̄
String Theory Layer
The fundamental object under price movement is a vibrating string. Charts are the visible trace — the surface imprint of deeper oscillations.
Strings

Price as Vibration

The fundamental object under price movement is a string. Price history (charts) is the visible trace of string vibration over time. Trends and patterns = interference outcomes.

chart = projection(vibration)
trend = interference pattern
Vertices

Vertices — Interaction Points

Strings interact through vertices. A vertex = a discrete interaction event (information, flow, impact) that changes vibration. Where energy transfers, splits, or connects.

vertex = event(info ∪ flow ∪ impact)
→ Δvibration
Nodes

Nodes — Standing Waves

Nodes = boundary/constraint points where motion is restricted or cancels. Create standing-wave behavior: repeated levels, magnet zones, structural boundaries.

node → standing wave
→ support/resistance (structural)
Waves

Wave Properties

Magnitude = strength/amplitude (energy expression). Frequency = how quickly states change (turbulence). Past charts are wave-traces — projections, not the full object.

A = amplitude (energy)
f = frequency (state switching)
Energy, Entropy & Dynamics
Energy is money in motion. Entropy is dispersion. The system always seeks balance, not stillness.
Energy

Energy = Money in Motion

Money is finite as stock, infinite as circulation. More energy → more accessible states → higher volatility → bracket expansion. Energy drives everything.

E = f(capital flow, leverage, credit)
E↑ → vol↑ → bracket↑
Entropy

Entropy — Dispersion of States

High entropy = disagreement, fragility. Low entropy = crowding, stability illusion. Entropy signals the system's internal coherence or lack thereof.

H = -Σ pᵢ log(pᵢ)
H↑ → fragility | H↓ → crowding
Balance

Balance & Brackets

Markets exist within dynamic brackets. Bullish and bearish forces in tension. Balance is sustained pressure, not equilibrium. Price is a range phenomenon.

balance = tension(bull, bear)
≠ equilibrium
Cooling

Cooling Mechanisms

Regulation, institutional strength, monetary policy, cost of capital. These dissipate energy naturally. When blocked (QE, bailouts, cheap credit), energy accumulates → pressure rises.

cooling blocked → ΔE accumulates
→ pressure → fragility
Fatigue

Fatigue & Bubbles

Fatigue: more energy produces less movement. Bubbles: energy is trapped, cannot dissipate. Expansion continues until structural failure. 2008 = canonical breach.

dP/dE → 0 (fatigue)
E trapped → bubble → breach
Crash

Crash / Breach Events

Structural failure, not emotional panic. Probability space collapses and reforms. The system fractures at geological level, not at surface narrative.

breach = structural fracture
P-space collapses → reforms
Geology, Ocean & Climate
Structural metaphors that connect deep system architecture to observable surface behavior.
Geology

Geology vs. Topography

Geology = structural value (deep, slow-moving). Topography = price (surface, fast-moving). Surface movement does not alter deep structure. Structure constrains surface.

geology → constrains → topography
Δsurface ≠ Δstructure
Ocean

Ocean & Climate Model

Capital flows behave as ocean currents. Temperature = optimism/fear. Volatility = turbulence. Deep currents (structural) move differently from surface waves (price).

current = capital flow
temp = sentiment | turb = volatility
Emergence

Micro → Macro Emergence

Micro rules generate macro behavior. No agent psychology required. Emergence replaces prediction. The ontology is multi-scale by design.

micro rules → macro patterns
prediction ← emergence
